## 1. Getting Started (Account Setup)

Before you can track your money, you need to create a secure account:

### Creating an Account (Register)
1. When you first open the app, you will land on the **Register** page.
2. Enter a unique **Username** (letters, numbers, and underscores only).
3. Provide your **Email Address** and choose a secure **Password** (minimum of 8 characters).
4. Re-enter your password in **Confirm Password** and click **Create Account**.
5. Once registered, the system will show a success message and redirect you to the Login screen.

### Logging In
1. On the **Login** page, type in your registered **Email Address** and **Password**.
2. Click **Sign In**.
3. You will be redirected to your personal **Dashboard**.

## 2. Managing Transactions (Income & Expenses)

The **Transactions** page acts as your financial ledger, keeping record of all money flowing in and out of your wallet.

### How to Add a Transaction
1. Navigate to the **Transactions** view using the sidebar (desktop) or bottom navigation bar (mobile).
2. Click the green **`+ Add Transaction`** button in the top right.
3. In the popup form:
   - Select **Expense** (money spent) or **Income** (allowance, salary, etc.) using the top toggle.
   - Input the **Amount** (must be greater than 0).
   - Choose a **Category** from the dropdown (e.g. *Food & Drinks*, *Allowance*, *Rent & Housing*).
   - Choose a **Date** (defaults to today).
   - Select your **Payment Method** (Cash, Debit Card, Credit Card, Digital Wallet, or Bank Transfer).
   - (Optional) Type a short note in the **Description** (e.g., *"Lunch with friends"*).
4. Click **Save Transaction**. The ledger and your dashboard balances will update instantly.

### How to Edit or Delete a Transaction
- **To Edit:** Find the transaction row in the ledger table. Click the **Edit (pencil icon)** on the right. Modify the details in the form and click **Save**.
- **To Delete:** Click the **Delete (trash can icon)** on the right of the transaction row. Click **OK** on the browser prompt to confirm.

### Filtering and Searching
You can easily find specific logs using the filters at the top of the Transactions page:
- Type words in the search bar to filter by description text.
- Filter results by selecting specific types (only show Expenses or only show Income).
- Filter by Category to inspect specific spending areas.
- Select Start and End dates to view transactions from a specific week or month.

## 3. Setting Category Budgets

Budgets prevent you from overspending by warning you when you spend too much on specific categories (like *Entertainment* or *Shopping*) during the month.

### How to Set a Budget Limit
1. Navigate to the **Budgets** page.
2. Click **`Set Budget Limit`** in the top right.
3. Select the **Target Category** you want to limit.
4. Input the maximum **Monthly Limit Amount** (e.g., `$150.00`).
5. Choose the **Start Date** and **End Date** for the budget cycle (defaults to the current month).
6. Click **Save Limit**.

### Monitoring your Budgets
The Budgets page displays a card for each active category limit showing your progress:
- **Green Progress Bar:** You have spent less than 80% of your budget. Everything is on track.
- **Amber Progress Bar:** You have spent between 80% and 100% of your budget. *Be cautious!*
- **Red Progress Bar & Warning Badge:** You have spent **100% or more** of your budget. The card will show exactly how much money you are over-budget.

## 4. Monitoring Savings Goals

Savings goals let you allocate and track money set aside for specific milestones (e.g., buying a laptop, building an emergency fund, or travel plans).

### How to Create a Savings Goal
1. Go to the **Savings Goals** page.
2. Click **`Create Savings Goal`** in the top right.
3. Enter a **Goal Name** (e.g. *"Macbook Pro Fund"*).
4. Enter your **Target Amount** (e.g. `$1,200.00`).
5. (Optional) Set a starting balance in **Starting Balance** and select a target deadline **Target Date**.
6. Click **Save Goal**.

### How to Deposit or Withdraw Money
Instead of manually editing the goal details, you can add or take out money dynamically:
1. Click on the **Savings Goal Card** you want to adjust.
2. In the adjustment modal:
   - Select **Deposit** (adding money) or **Withdraw** (taking out money).
   - Enter the **Amount**.
   - Click **Confirm**.
3. The card progress bar and percentage tracker will update. When your current savings match or exceed the target, the card will display a green **Completed** badge.

## 5. Understanding Analytics & Reports

The **Analytics** page converts your numbers into interactive charts to give you deep insights into your financial behavior.

### Reading the Visual Charts
- **Account Box Metrics:** Shows your overall lifetime balance (total income minus total expenses), current range income, current range expenses, and your **Savings Rate** (what percentage of your income you managed to keep).
- **Cash Flow Trend (Line Chart):** Compares your Income vs. Expenses over the last 6 months. Hover over the chart nodes to view exact values for each month.
- **Expense Allocation (Doughnut Chart):** Displays a color-coded percentage breakdown of where your money goes. Hover over the slices to see exactly how much you spent on each category.

### Changing the Reporting Date Range
By default, the analytics charts load data for the **current month**. You can change the dates in the **Reporting Range** panel at the top of the page to inspect custom time ranges (e.g., last week, last quarter, or a specific semester).

### Exporting your Ledger (CSV Download)
If you want to view your transactions in Excel or Google Sheets:
1. Go to the **Analytics** page.
2. Click the **`Export Ledger (CSV)`** button in the top right.
3. A spreadsheet file named `kuwenta_report_YYYY-MM-DD.csv` will automatically download to your computer.

## 6. Preferences & Profile Configurations

### Adjusting Settings
1. Go to the **Settings** page.
2. **Change Currency:** Select your preferred currency from the dropdown (USD `$`, PHP `₱`, EUR `€`, GBP `£`). This updates the currency symbol across your entire dashboard instantly.
3. **Appearance Mode:** Click the **Toggle Theme** button to switch between Dark Mode and Light Mode.

### Profile Check
1. Go to the **Profile** page.
2. Here you can inspect your account details, email address, and view your account creation date.
